About Me

Hi, I'm Amelia

I am a junior at the University of Maryland studying multiplatform journalism. I have contributed to three different publications during college, and I now work as a multimedia reporter for The Diamondback, an independent campus publication. I am interested in all reporting mediums, but most excited about video storytelling. I have created multiple video projects with "crude" equipment because I believe that the best way to work towards my end goal of producing documentaries is to pick up my camera.
